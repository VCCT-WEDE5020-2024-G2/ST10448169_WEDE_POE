Latest News from Ecolight

Welcome to the Ecolight Newsroom! Stay updated with the latest developments, projects, and innovations in the renewable energy sector. Here, you'll find articles, press releases, and updates that highlight our ongoing efforts to lead the charge towards a sustainable future.

New Solar Farm Launch in Renewable City

Date: August 10, 2024

Ecolight is proud to announce the launch of our latest solar farm in Renewable City. This state-of-the-art facility will provide clean energy to over 10,000 homes, furthering our commitment to reducing carbon emissions and promoting sustainable energy solutions.

Ecolight Partners with GreenTech for Wind Energy Research

Date: July 28, 2024

In an exciting collaboration, Ecolight has partnered with GreenTech to advance wind energy research. This partnership aims to develop more efficient wind turbines, helping to increase the viability and scalability of wind power as a renewable energy source.

Sustainability Education Program Expands to 50 New Schools

Date: July 15, 2024

Our Sustainability Education Program continues to grow! We are thrilled to announce that the program has been expanded to include 50 new schools across the country, reaching more young minds and fostering a new generation of environmental stewards.

Ecolight Receives Award for Innovation in Renewable Energy

Date: June 30, 2024

Ecolight has been honored with the Innovation in Renewable Energy Award for our groundbreaking work in solar energy storage. This award recognizes our commitment to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the renewable energy industry.
